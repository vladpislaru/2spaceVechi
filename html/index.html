About Us

STUDENTS2SPACE is an NGO that wants to provide practical contexts for students passionate about the space industry, especially rockets. Specifically, this initiative supports volunteers in building and launching various rocket models. STUDENTS2SPACE main activity takes place inside the Research Center CAMPUS within University POLITEHNICA of Bucharest, Romania.

EuRoC is the biggest rocketry contest that takes place in the EU and the second largest competition globally. EuRoC seeks to stimulate engineering students to design, build and launch their own vehicles.(https://euroc.pt/)

In a broader spectrum the competition aims to encourage students to study science, technology, engineering and math (STEM) and to the development of technological skills.

Last edition, we were proud to be one of the twenty teams that were selected around the world and the first Romanian team of students & engineers that got accepted. This edition, joining EuRoC again will be an amazing opportunity for us to show the world what we managed to develop during the past year!

The goal of STUDENTS2SPACE in EuRoC 2022 is to reach 9000m with its own developed rocket. Thus, members will gain experience in building the components of the rocket structure and electronics.
