First competition of PocketQube satellite prototypes in the world
2SPACE team together with the POLITEHNICA University of Bucharest organised the first competition of PocketQube satellite prototypes in the world, with the purpose to engage as many high school students passionate about space as possible, to be helped and guided in order to succeed in a still small field in Romania.
The students, the coordinating professors and the people from the national space field were delighted with the initiative, and the workshop part was a unique experience for both the team and the participants.
The competition represents a one of a kind opportunity for young people in Romania who are passionate about a field that is in constant development. Considering the success enjoyed within the event’s first edition, 2SPACE team hopes that this competition will become a tradition both for the association and for the POLITEHNICA University of Bucharest, in an attempt to increase the interest of young pupils and students, but also of future generations in the aerospace field.
